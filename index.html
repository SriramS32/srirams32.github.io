Sriram Somasundaram

I am currently a M.S. candidate in CS at Stanford University. I was an undergraduate researcher at USC's Cognitive Learning for Vision and Robotics Lab and was advised by Prof. Joseph J. Lim. My research interests are reinforcement learning, robotics, and machine learning.

In 2015, I was a research intern at the Mellins Lab at Stanford University under Prof. Elizabeth Mellins. I've also interned at Riot Games, Raytheon, and Quid.

Research

I'm interested in investigating how learning algorithms can enable machines to acquire complex skills in real-world settings. My research experiences thus far delve into learning and interpreting deep representations for complex skills and enabling machines to smoothly compose and execute skills for hierarchical tasks.
